Stand and Conquer

Welcome to Wheatless Warfare, your ultimate resource for navigating life without gluten! Whether you’re newly diagnosed, a seasoned veteran of celiac disease or gluten intolerant, you’re in the right place. Our mission is to provide safe, efficient, and practical eating solutions for home, school, work, and dining out. Every tip, recipe, and resource has been rigorously tested by me, a severe celiac, ensuring you receive reliable information. With detailed notes included, you can assess what works best for your unique needs. This easy-to-navigate site is designed to make your gluten-free journey as smooth as possible. Let’s conquer this together!

Who is Wheatless Warfare?

Hi! I'm Maggie, a diagnosed celiac with severe symptoms trying to navigate life in the Gluten-Free world. While navigating my life, I've realized that there aren't a whole lot of easy, simple, and resourceful places to learn how to be safely Gluten-Free. Don't get me wrong, there's tons of cool websites with food recommendations, but none really help with the lifestyle change. That's what this site is for! Here I'll try to simplify dealing with:

  • Cross Contamination
  • Eating Out
  • How to Cook Safely
  • How to Cook on a Budget
  • Where to Shop with Actual Good Products
  • Being a Proud Confident Gluten-Free Person

I'm excited to share my knowledge with all of you and hope that it all helps with your journey however far along you are on it!
